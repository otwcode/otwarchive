Support the OTW

Thank you for supporting the Organization for Transformative Works.

Donors at the US$50 level or above are eligible for thank-you gifts. While we're experiencing difficulties with our main donation page, you can visit the official OTW Tumblr for a list of all available gifts. If you donate through one of the options below, the Development & Membership team will contact you to confirm your gift choice.

All the information you enter during the payment process is completely separate from your AO3 account. We cannot and will not link your personal information to your activity on the Archive of Our Own, ever.

If you have questions about donating, please e-mail the Development & Membership committee at devmem [at] transformativeworks [dot] org.

Membership donations

You can become a member of the OTW by donating at least US$10, or the equivalent in another currency. Members have the right to vote in OTW elections. Membership lasts for one year from the date of the donation; if you are already a member, donate now and your membership will be extended to one year from your most recent donation date.

If you would like to become an OTW member, please use this button.

Non-membership donations

If you would prefer to donate without becoming an OTW member, or if you are donating on behalf of a corporation, please use this button.
