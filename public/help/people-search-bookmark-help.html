People Search Number of Bookmarks

You can find people with a specific number of bookmarks:

  • 5: a single number will find that exact amount
  • <100: will find less than that amount
  • >100: will find more than that amount
  • 100-1000: will find in the range of 100 to 1000, inclusive
  • Periods and commas are ignored: 1.000 = 1,000 = 1000
